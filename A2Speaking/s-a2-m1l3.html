Word list:


📌 be a fan of
📌 be fond of
📌 be interested in
📌 be into
📌 can't stand

Example:


- I'm fond of jogging.
- How often do you go jogging?
- About four times a week.
- When do you find the time to go?
- I usually go in the morning before I go to work.
- What about you?
- Oh, I hate jogging. I find it boring.
