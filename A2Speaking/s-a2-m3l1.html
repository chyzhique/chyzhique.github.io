Speaking task:

☐ Person A: Imagine that you are the check-in clerk and that your partner is a passenger checking in his/her luggage.

☐ Person B: Imagine that you are a passenger checking in your luggage and that your partner is a check-in clerk.

☐ Discuss and use the prompts in the box to ask questions and give answers.
!
Before you speak, make sure you understand the task and how you should use the prompts

Person A:

📌 where / travelling / to?
📌 passport and ticket / please?
📌 how many / bags/ check in?
📌 put on / conveyor belt / please?

Person B:

📌 have / aisle-window seat / please?
📌 check in / this bag / too?
📌 take / laptop, tablet, etc. / on plane?

Example:

- Morning, where are you travelling to? - Los Angeles. It's a long trip, may I have a window seat, please?...
