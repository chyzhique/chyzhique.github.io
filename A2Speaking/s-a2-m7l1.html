Speaking game:

☐ Read the following situations and act out of them.

Situation 1

📌 Person A: you are a bank clerk and Person B is a customer who wants to withdraw some money.

Situation 2

📌 Person A: you are in a bureau de change and you want to exchange some money. Talk to Person B.

Situation 3

📌 Person A: you're passing by a cash machine. Person B is there and needs some help. Tell him/her how to use it.
