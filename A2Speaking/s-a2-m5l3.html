Speaking play:

☐ Student A: Imagine that you have a problem, either physical or psychological.
Tell Student B how you feel and ask him/her for advice. Use some of the ideas in the box.
☐ Student B: Listen to Student Ns problem and give him/her advice. Use should(n 't) and had better (not) and some of the ideas in the box.

A's problem:

📌 can't sleep at night
📌 often panic
📌 suffer from stress
📌 feel tired all the time
📌 have a phobia

B's advice:

📌 do physical activity
📌 drink warm milk
📌 take deep breaths
📌 work less
📌 see doctor/psychologist
📌 go on holiday
📌 take vitamins/medication

Example:

- I feel terrible.
- Why? What's wrong?
- I can't sleep at night. Could you help me?
- Well, I think you should/shouldn't ...
