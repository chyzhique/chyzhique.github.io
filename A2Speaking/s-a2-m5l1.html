Situation 1:

📌 A: Imagine that you are at the doctor's surgery. B is the doctor. Describe and discuss your problem with him/her and ask for advice.
📌 B: Imagine that you are a doctor. Listen to Student A's problem, ask him/her anything you want to know and tell him/her what to do.
