Speaking game:

☐ Think of a show or make one up.

☐ Think of things to say about it

☐ Advertise it to your partner.

☐ Use the questions below.

Questions:



📌 How long has it been running?
📌 How long has it been touring?
📌 Which places has it toured so far?
📌 What age group is the show for?
📌 What is it about?
📌 How have audiences responded?
📌 Where can you get tickets?
📌 How much are they?
