Speaking task:

☐ Think of an adventurous experience you once had.
☐ Imagine you are a reporter and ask questions using the prompts below.
☐ Give answers

Background:

📌 when it happened
📌 where he/she was
📌 what he/she was doing
📌 who was with him/her

Gist:

📌 what happened
📌 how he/she felt
📌 what he/she did
📌 what happened then

Example:

- So what is the most adventurous experience you ever had?
- I take a spontaneous road trip to the nearest forest.
- When did it happen?
- Last summer when I was on holidays.
- What did you do?
- I saw a hidden waterfall and had barbecue with my friends.
