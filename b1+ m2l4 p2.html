Cheese rolling

There are many strange customs and traditions all over the world. Last week I the opportunity to witness the famous cheese rolling custom which takes in different parts of England. A round cheese is rolled down a hill and the competitors run after . The winner is the first person to grab the cheese. Coopers Hill is the famous cheese rolling site. When I arrived there, the men and women already gathered on the top of the hill. When the cheese was dropped, everyone started racing down a nearly vertical slope. It spectacular to watch but much too dangerous to take part in. Many of the competitors ended up with broken arms and legs. After the competition, I asked one the participants, who was all bandaged up, if it had worth it and he replied that it was the most fun he had ever had!
