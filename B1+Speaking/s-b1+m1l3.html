"courageous;"

Courageous describes someone who is brave, willing to face difficult or dangerous situations without fear.

Synonyms: brave, fearless, valiant

"clever;"

Clever describes someone who is intelligent, quick-witted, and able to think or solve problems effectively.

Synonyms: intelligent, smart, sharp

"strong;"

Strong describes someone who has physical or mental power, is physically fit, or possesses great force or influence.

Synonyms: powerful, robust, sturdy

"fast;"

Fast describes something that moves or occurs with great speed or velocity.

Synonyms: quick, speedy, rapid

"popular;"

Popular describes something or someone who is well-liked, widely accepted, or enjoyed by many people.

Synonyms: well-liked, favored, admired
