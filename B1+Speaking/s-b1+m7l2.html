What the Options are:
1) laser eye surgery: He can have laser eye surgery to get his vision corrected. It's a fast (about 10 mins), painless procedure with a uick recovery. However, not all eyesight problems can be cured with laser eye surgery and results may vary from patient to patient. It is also quite expensive;
2) exploring the wilderness: He can start wearing contact lenses. Contact lenses move with your eye and have no frames to obstruct vision. There is high risk of eye infection if contact lenses are not cleaned with appropriate solution and stored properly.
