"designer labels;"

Designer labels refer to high-end fashion brands known for their exclusive and often expensive clothing and accessories.

Synonyms: fashion brands, luxury labels, high-end designers

"sales;"

Sales are events or promotions in which products are offered at discounted prices, typically to attract customers and increase purchases.

Synonyms: discounts, deals, promotions

"street market;"

A street market is an outdoor marketplace where vendors sell a variety of goods, often including fresh produce, crafts, and street food.

Synonyms: open-air market, flea market, bazaar

"air conditioning;"

Air conditioning is a system or technology that cools and conditions indoor air to maintain a comfortable temperature, especially during hot weather.

Synonyms: AC, cooling, climate control

"bargain;"

A bargain is a purchase or deal in which a buyer obtains goods or services at a lower cost than usual or at a favorable price.

Synonyms: deal, discount, steal

"affordable;"

Affordable means reasonably priced or within one's budget, making it accessible to a wide range of consumers.

Synonyms: reasonably priced, economical, budget-friendly

"better quality;"

Better quality refers to products or items that are made with higher standards and materials, often resulting in superior performance or durability.

Synonyms: higher quality, superior, improved

"credit card;"

A credit card is a plastic card that allows the cardholder to make purchases on credit, with the promise to pay the balance at a later date.

Synonyms: charge card, payment card, plastic money

"department store;"

A department store is a large retail establishment that offers a wide range of products and merchandise across various departments and categories.

Synonyms: big store, retail emporium, shopping center
