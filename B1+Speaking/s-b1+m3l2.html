"valuable experience;"

Valuable experience refers to knowledge and skills gained through practical exposure or learning, often considered beneficial or important.

Synonyms: valuable knowledge, practical skills, beneficial learning

"make ends meet;"

Make ends meet means to manage one's finances in a way that covers all necessary expenses with available resources.

Synonyms: balance the budget, meet financial obligations, manage expenses

"flexible;"

Flexible describes the ability to adapt to changing circumstances, situations, or requirements with ease.

Synonyms: adaptable, versatile, adjustable

"greater independence;"

Greater independence refers to an increased level of self-sufficiency, autonomy, or freedom to make decisions and take actions.

Synonyms: increased autonomy, greater self-sufficiency, enhanced freedom

"responsible;"

Responsible describes someone who is accountable, dependable, and capable of fulfilling duties or obligations.

Synonyms: accountable, dependable, reliable

"efficient;"

Efficient describes the ability to achieve maximum productivity or results with minimal wasted effort or resources.

Synonyms: productive, effective, resourceful

"communication skills;"

Communication skills refer to the ability to convey information, ideas, or feelings effectively to others through verbal and non-verbal means.

Synonyms: interpersonal skills, speaking abilities, effective communication
