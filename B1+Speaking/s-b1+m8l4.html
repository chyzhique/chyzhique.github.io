"surf the Net;"

Surf the Net means to browse or navigate the internet, often to search for information, websites, or engage in online activities.

Synonyms: browse the web, explore the internet, online surfing

"download (pictures, music, etc.);"

Download means to transfer files, such as pictures, music, or documents, from the internet or another source to a computer or device for offline use.

Synonyms: retrieve, save, transfer

"widely used;"

Widely used means that something is commonly or extensively employed, utilized, or adopted by a large number of people or in various contexts.

Synonyms: commonly used, extensively adopted, widely applied

"get informed;"

Get informed means to acquire knowledge or information about a particular topic, issue, or event by seeking out and gathering relevant details.

Synonyms: become knowledgeable, stay informed, get up to date
