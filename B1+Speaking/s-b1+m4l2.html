"microscope;"

A microscope is a scientific instrument used for magnifying and studying small objects or organisms that are not visible to the naked eye.

Synonyms: optical instrument, magnifier, scientific microscope

"test tubes;"

Test tubes are cylindrical glass or plastic containers used in scientific experiments to hold and mix liquids or substances.

Synonyms: laboratory tubes, glass tubes, experimental vials

"breakthrough;"

A breakthrough is a significant discovery or development that represents a major advance or success in a particular field or endeavor.

Synonyms: discovery, advancement, milestone

"experiment;"

An experiment is a systematic procedure carried out to test a hypothesis, demonstrate a principle, or gather data and information in a controlled setting.

Synonyms: test, trial, investigation

"research;"

Research is the process of systematically studying and investigating a topic, problem, or question in order to gain knowledge and understanding.

Synonyms: study, investigation, exploration

"laboratory;"

A laboratory is a controlled environment equipped with specialized equipment and tools for conducting scientific experiments, research, and analysis.

Synonyms: lab, research facility, scientific workspace
