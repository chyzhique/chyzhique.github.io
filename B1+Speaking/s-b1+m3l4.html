"authentic texts;"

Authentic texts are real, original written or spoken materials that reflect natural language usage and are not modified for language learning purposes.

Synonyms: genuine texts, unaltered materials, real documents

"read extensively;"

Read extensively means to read a wide variety of materials extensively or in great quantities, often for the purpose of improving language skills or knowledge.

Synonyms: read widely, read extensively, read in large quantities

"audio-visual material;"

Audio-visual material refers to content that combines both audio and visual elements, such as videos, presentations, or multimedia resources.

Synonyms: multimedia content, audiovisual materials, mixed media

"travel abroad;"

Travel abroad means to journey to foreign countries or destinations outside of one's home country for various purposes, including leisure, education, or business.

Synonyms: international travel, overseas trips, foreign journeys

"individual attention;"

Individual attention means personalized or one-on-one focus and assistance given to a person's specific needs or requirements.

Synonyms: personal attention, customized support, individualized care

"focused pace;"

Focused pace refers to the speed or tempo of learning or progress that is specifically tailored to the needs and objectives of the individual or group.

Synonyms: targeted speed, customized tempo, specific pace

"interaction progress;"

Interaction progress describes the advancement or improvement of communication and engagement among individuals or within a group.

Synonyms: communication development, interaction improvement, progress in engagement

"computer programs;"

Computer programs are sets of instructions or software applications designed to perform specific tasks or functions on a computer.

Synonyms: software, applications, computer applications
