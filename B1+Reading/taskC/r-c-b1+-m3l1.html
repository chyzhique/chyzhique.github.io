GUESSING THE MEANING OF UNKNOWN WORDS

Look at the following words from the text and choose the correct meaning a, b or c.
essential a. unnecessary b. important c. exciting
baggy a. tight b. small c. loose
identify a. understand b. arrest c. say who somebody is
convicted a. found guilty b. set free c. arrested
glamorous a. unusual b. attractive and exciting c. well-paid
