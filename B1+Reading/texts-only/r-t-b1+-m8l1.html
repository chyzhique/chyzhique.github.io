GSP GARDEN STATE PLAZA

Located in Paramus, New Jersey, it is the largest shopping mall in the New York City metropolitan area, and one of the largest malls in the whole US. It has many stores which cater for both the big and small spender including Macy's, Lord and Taylor's, and Nordstrom's. It is conveniently located just across the highway from large outlets for the home like IKEA, Bed, Bath and Beyond and Sports Authority. This doubles its appeal to weekend shoppers, who want to have access to a range of products from high-end clothing and cosmetics, to furniture and sporting equipment. Transportation to the mall is never a problem, as there are bus lines that go directly to and from the mall to downtown New York.

Hours: Monday - Saturday 10am - 9:30pm

Short Hills mall

The Mall at Short Hills, more commonly known as The Short Hills Mall, is a high-end shopping mall and is one of the largest in the state. One of the best features is the location. It is located in Milburn, New Jersey, which is less than 30 miles away from Manhattan. There is also a Hilton Hotel right across the street for out-of-towners who don't want to drive back home. Shoppers feel as though they entered a world of luxury when they come to Short Hills, because the mall is decorated with fountains, sculptures, marble floors and has many lounges for relaxing with leather furniture and plasma TVs. Several times a year, the mall hosts luxury car shows, which display extravagant automobiles like Bentleys, Porsches and Land Rovers. Pier Village

Pier Village is located right on the Atlantic Ocean, in Long Branch, New Jersey. This shopping mall has a variety of stores but has many other attractions as well. For example, there is even an amusement park with traditional rides such as roller coasters and a Ferris wheel. The first storey of the mall is full of stores and restaurants. Many of the stores are locally-run businesses, but there are many national chains, such as Stewart's Root Beer and The Rocky Mountain Chocolate Factory. The upper floors are where the luxury ocean- view apartments are located. The spa, beauty salon and gym are also very popular with mall goers. During the summer, there is live music
