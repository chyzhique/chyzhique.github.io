"learning environment;"

A learning environment refers to the physical, social, and psychological setting in which learning takes place.

Synonyms: educational setting, instructional context, study environment

"presence of teacher;"

The presence of a teacher indicates the availability of an instructor or educator to guide and support the learning process.

Synonyms: teacher's presence, teacher's involvement, instructor's presence

"lively discussion;"

A lively discussion is an active and engaging conversation involving multiple participants, often characterized by the exchange of ideas and viewpoints.

Synonyms: animated conversation, spirited discussion, lively debate

"contact with other students;"

Contact with other students involves interactions and communication with fellow learners, fostering collaboration and social learning.

Synonyms: interaction with peers, communication with classmates, social engagement

"pairwork / group work;"

Pairwork or group work involves collaborative activities where students work together in pairs or groups to achieve a common goal or complete a task.

Synonyms: collaborative tasks, team projects, group assignments

"ask questions;"

Asking questions is a fundamental learning practice where students seek clarification, knowledge, or information by posing inquiries to their instructors or peers.

Synonyms: inquire, seek information, request clarification

"peaceful atmosphere;"

A peaceful atmosphere refers to a calm and tranquil environment conducive to focused and effective learning without distractions or disruptions.

Synonyms: tranquil environment, serene ambiance, quiet setting

"work at own speed;"

Working at one's own speed allows students to progress through learning materials and tasks at a pace that suits their individual learning preferences and needs.

Synonyms: self-paced learning, individual pace, personalized learning

"convenient;"

Convenient learning implies flexibility and ease of access to educational resources and opportunities, accommodating learners' schedules and preferences.

Synonyms: user-friendly, accessible, hassle-free

"immediate feedback;"

Immediate feedback provides learners with prompt and constructive responses on their performance, facilitating learning and improvement.

Synonyms: prompt response, quick assessment, instant evaluation

"motivation interaction;"

Motivation interaction involves engaging and inspiring interactions that stimulate students' motivation and enthusiasm for learning.

Synonyms: inspiring engagement, motivation-boosting interaction, enthusiasm stimulation
