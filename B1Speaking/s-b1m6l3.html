"convenient;"

Convenient describes something that is easy to use, suitable for one's needs, or saves time and effort.

Synonyms: user-friendly, handy, time-saving

"keep fit;"

Keep fit means to engage in activities or exercises that promote physical health and fitness.

Synonyms: stay in shape, maintain fitness, exercise regularly

"safe;"

Safe describes a condition or situation that is free from harm, danger, or risk of injury.

Synonyms: secure, protected, out of harm's way

"comfortable;"

Comfortable describes a state of physical or mental ease and contentment, without discomfort or stress.

Synonyms: cozy, relaxed, at ease

"tiring;"

Tiring describes something that causes fatigue or exhaustion due to physical or mental effort.

Synonyms: exhausting, draining, wearisome

"expensive fuel;"

Expensive fuel refers to fuel or energy sources that come at a high cost, often in terms of money or environmental impact.

Synonyms: costly fuel, high-priced energy

"parking problem;"

A parking problem refers to difficulties or challenges in finding a suitable place to park a vehicle.

Synonyms: parking challenges, parking difficulties

"exhaust fumes;"

Exhaust fumes are the gases and pollutants emitted from the exhaust systems of vehicles and machinery, often contributing to air pollution.

Synonyms: vehicle emissions, exhaust gases, tailpipe fumes

"exposed to heat/rain/snow;"

Exposed to heat/rain/snow means being subjected to extreme weather conditions, such as high temperatures, rain, or snow.

Synonyms: subjected to weather, faced with elements

"traffic jams;"

Traffic jams are situations where traffic comes to a halt or moves very slowly due to congestion on roads or highways.

Synonyms: gridlock, traffic congestion, road blockage

"air/noise pollution;"

Air/noise pollution refers to excessive, unwanted, or harmful levels of air or noise contaminants that disrupt the environment and affect human health.

Synonyms: sound pollution, environmental noise, air contamination

"environmentally friendly;"

Environmentally friendly describes products, practices, or actions that are designed to have minimal impact on the environment and promote sustainability.

Synonyms: eco-friendly, green, sustainable
