"household chores;"

Household chores refer to the tasks and duties related to maintaining and cleaning a home, such as cooking, cleaning, and laundry.

Synonyms: domestic tasks, housework, home maintenance

"share responsibility;"

Share responsibility means to distribute tasks and duties among individuals or group members to collectively manage a task or obligation.

Synonyms: divide responsibility, distribute duties, cooperate in tasks

"take turns;"

Take turns means to alternate or share the opportunity to do something with others in a sequence or rotation.

Synonyms: alternate, rotate, switch

"duty;"

Duty refers to a moral or legal obligation, responsibility, or task that one is expected or required to fulfill.

Synonyms: obligation, responsibility, task

"alone;"

Alone describes a state of being by oneself, without others or in solitude.

Synonyms: solitary, by oneself, solo

"lonely;"

Lonely describes a feeling of isolation or solitude, often accompanied by a desire for companionship or social interaction.

Synonyms: isolated, alone, desolate

"isolated;"

Isolated describes a state of being separated or cut off from others, often resulting in feelings of loneliness or exclusion.

Synonyms: secluded, remote, disconnected

"make friends;"

Make friends means to form new social connections and establish relationships with other people.

Synonyms: befriend, form relationships, socialize
