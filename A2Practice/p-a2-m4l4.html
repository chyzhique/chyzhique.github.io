Prepositions

Complete the dialogues with who, which, that or where.

1.
A: ls that the laptop (1) you bought last week?
B: Yeah.
A: It's nice. Where did you get it from?
B: From the shop (2) you used to work. In fact, the man (3) sold it to me knew you.
A: Did he? What was his name?
B: Alan Bishop.
A: Oh, he was one of the people (4) I hung out with while I was there.
2.
A: Denise, can I borrow your green top?
B: Which one?
A: The one (5) you were wearing yesterday.
B: Go on then. Are you going out?
A: Yeah, I'm meeting Laura. You know, the girl (6) is in my computer class.
B: Where are you going?
A: To Ricky's.
B: Where?
A: You know, the cafe (7) is in the park. It's the place (8) her sister works.
B: Have fun!
