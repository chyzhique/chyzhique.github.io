PHRASES/EXPRESSIONS WITH 'MOST' AND 'LEAST'

a. at all
b. more than anything else
c. especially not
d. not less than
e. not more than
f. as important as the rest
g. not to say everything that you could say
h. get the maximum advantage from
  1. I thought the film would be scary but I wasn't in the least frightened.
  2. Children should drink at least two glasses of milk per day.
  3. I want to thank my friends Peter, Nate, Kelly and last but not least Carrie for all their support.
  4. Nobody believed her story, least of all the police.
  5. It's an awful restaurant; the food is tasteless to say the least.
  6. I plan to make the most of my holiday and spend my time relaxing.
  7. We will stay in London for ten days at most.
  8. The thing I liked most of all about the film was the music.
