Paraphrasing

Complete the Sentences

  1. Katie regrets not flying to Paris to see her family.
    [wishes]
    Katie to Paris to see her family.
  2. Susan and Alex hate heavy metal music, so I don't think they will go to the concert.
    [nor]
    Neither metal music, so I don't think they will go to the concert.
  3. Derek woke up very late so he missed the interview.
    [woken]
    If Derek he wouldn't have missed the interview.
  4. I can't buy that car because it is very expensive.
    [only]
    If so expensive.
  5. I invited my colleagues to dinner but nobody was able to come.
    [all]
    I invited my colleagues to dinner unable to come.
  6. Rill didn't receive an invitation, so he didn't go to the wedding.
    [have]
    Bill the wedding if he had received an invitation.
  7. I live in the suburbs but I prefer the city centre.
    [lived]
    If city centre instead of the suburbs.
  8. Sarah sold her flat and now she regrets it.
    [had]
    Sarah her flat.
