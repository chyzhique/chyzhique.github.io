WRITING TASK

Write your own CV.

❗When writing a CV:

  • remember to be brief and to the point.
  • focus on your abilities and strong points which are relevant to the job you are applying for.
  • don't write information that is unnecessary (e.g. appearance, irrelevant awards).
  • don't write full sentences (e.g. write 'good knowledge of Italian' rather than 'I can speak Italian well
  • group the information in separate sections (personal information, work experience, education, personal skills, interests, references).
  • do not use informal language.
  • highlight important information in bold.
