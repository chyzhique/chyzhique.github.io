INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Melt the butter, but be careful not to cook it
  2. Put the butter in a bowl, add the sugar and the eggs and whisk
  3. Slowly add the the cocoa powder and whisk some more
  4. Put the bisquit in a large bowl and break them with a back of a spoon
  5. Pour the mixture into the bowl with the bisquit scrumbs and stir until it is mixed well.
  6. Place this mixture on a piece of aluminium foil and shape it like a salami
  7. Wrap it up in the aluminium foil and put it in the fridge for a couple of hours
  8. Slice your chocolate salami and serve it with coffee or tea

(serves 8)

INGREDIENTS

  • 300g biscuits
