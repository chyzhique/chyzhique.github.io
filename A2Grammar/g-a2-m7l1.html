  • Question tags are short questions which we put at the /

  • They are formed with / verb (am, is, are, was, were, have, has, do, does, did, can, could, will, etc.) and a subject personal pronoun (I, you, he, she, etc.).

  • We use a question tag with a negative sentence and a question tag with a positive sentence.
