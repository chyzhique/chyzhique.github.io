DIRECT SPEECH

Sue: 'Why is Dave angry with me?

Sue asked why Dave was angry with her.

REPORTED SPEECH
(commands-requests)

Read the examples and complete the rule.

DIRECT SPEECH

Steve: 'Please don't interrupt me. Be quiet!'

REPORTED SPEECH

Steve asked me not to interrupt him. He told me to be quiet.
