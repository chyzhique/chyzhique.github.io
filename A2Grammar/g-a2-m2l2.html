PAST SIMPLE vs PAST PROGRESSIVE

TIME CLAUSES
(WHEN, WHILE, AS, AS SOON AS)

A. Read the examples and notice the words in bold. Then match them with a-c.

1. While I was travelling on the underground, I saw a strange sign.
  1. The two actions were happening at the same time.
  2. The two actions happened one after the other.
  3. One action was in progress when the other happened.
2. I was looking at the merchant while he was cutting the fruit in half.
3. As soon as I finished eating the piece, the merchant offered me some more.
