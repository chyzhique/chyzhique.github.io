TIME CLAUSES
(WHEN, WHILE, AS, AS SOON AS)

A. Read the examples and notice the words in bold. Then match them with a-c.

1. While I was travelling on the underground, I saw a strange sign. a. The two actions were happening at the same time.
2. I was looking at the merchant while he was cutting the fruit in half. b. The two actions happened one after the other.
3. As soon as I finished eating the piece, the merchant offered me some more. c. One action was in progress when the other happened.
