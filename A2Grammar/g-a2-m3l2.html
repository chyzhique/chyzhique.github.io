HAVE TO, DON'T HAVE TO, MUST, MUSTN'T, NEED TO, DON'T NEED TO, NEEDN'T

Read the examples and decide what each of them means. Choose a, b or c.

1. You have to be physically fit.

2. You don't have to wear special clothing.

3. Each person must have a headlamp.

4. You mustn't go without an experienced caver.

5. You need to have safety equipment.

6. Experienced cavers don't need to take a training course.

7. You needn't buy special clothing.
