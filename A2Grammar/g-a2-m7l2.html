EXCLAMATORY SENTENCES

Read the examples below. Which words does the speaker want to emphasise?

• How thrilling!    • What a treat!    • It's so pleasant!  

• It's such a wonderful place!


To make exclamations use:

• how and so before adjectives/adverbs
• what and such before nouns or adjectives + nouns

To express result use:

- so + adjective or adverb + (that) ...

- such + (a/an) + (adjective) + object + (that) ...
