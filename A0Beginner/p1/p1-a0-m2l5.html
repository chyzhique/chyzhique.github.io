A. Look at the picture from the film Hairspray. Who do you think the actor is?/

  Hairspray is a film about a girl called Tracy Turnblad. Tracy is chubby but she's a great dancer.
 In the film, John Travolta plays Edna Turnblad, Tracy's mother. Of course, he is very different. Edna is overweight and she's got medium-length hair.
 In real life, John Travolta isn't overweight and he's got short dark hair. Also, Travolta's eyes are blue, but in the film Edna's got brown eyes.
 It's a big difference.
