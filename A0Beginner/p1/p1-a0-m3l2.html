A. What do you think the two girls are talking about? Listen, read and find out.

Anna Hi, Sophie. Are you ready?
Sophie Give me a minute.
Anna Do you go jogging only on Sundays?
Sophie No, I don't. I go on Saturdays, too. What about you?
Anna I only go jogging on Sundays. You see, I'm not very active. I watch a lot of YouTube videos and I read magazines.
Sophie What else do you do in your free time?
Anna I go to the gym and I play tennis.
Sophie When do you play?
Anna I play on Tuesdays and Thursdays after work.
Sophie OK. Ready now?
Anna Yep. Let's go!
