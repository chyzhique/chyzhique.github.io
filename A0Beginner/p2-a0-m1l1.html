Listening

E. Listen and choose the correct answer.

  1. a. Good morning.
    b. Very well, thanks.
  2. a. Have a nice weekend.
    b. Fine thanks. And you?
  3. a. I'm Steven.
    b. Not bad.
  4. a. Bye!
    b. I'm OK.
  5. a. Take care.
    b. What's up?
  6. a. See you tomorrow!
    b. Great!
