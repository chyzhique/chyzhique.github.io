B. Write the correct words to complete the rules below.

• When we use the question words who, what, which, how much and how many to ask about the subject of the verb, the verb in the question form.
• With other question words (when, where, why, etc.), and when we use the question words like what, which, how much and how many to ask about the object of the verb, the verb in the question form.
