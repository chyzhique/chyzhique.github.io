A. Read the examples below and choose the correct words to complete the rules.

✏️ The sun is a star, not a planet.

✏️ The authorities hope that the birds will breed and that the number of kakapo will increase in the coming years.

✏️ Komodo Island has been turned into a national park. Hunting the dragons is forbidden in the park.

- We use   before a singular countable noun when we refer to it in a general sense or when it is mentioned for the first time

- We use   before a noun which is something unique, is used in a specific sense or has been mentioned before.

NOTE

Before a noun which represents a species we can use a(n), the or the plural form.

The penguin can't fly.
A penguin can't fly.
Penguins can't fly.
