... I asked him why it had been important to keep a record...
... When I asked him if he would go on such a difficult expedition again...

• What questions did the reporter actually ask Jason?
• Which words do the reported questions begin with?

We commonly introduce reported questions with ask, wonder and want to know.

2. REPORTED COMMANDS AND REQUESTS

A. Read the dialogues below and answer the questions that follow.

Trainer: Stretch your arms, please.
Sue: What did he say?
Jim: He asked us to stretch our arms.
Trainer: Don't be lazy, Harriet.
Sue: What did he say to Harriet?
Jim: He told her not to be lazy.

• Which verb is used to report a command?
• Which verb is used to report a request?
• What does the imperative (e.g. Stretch) change to in Reported Speech?
• What about the negative imperative (e.g. Don't be)?
