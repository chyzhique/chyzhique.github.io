B. Rewrite the sentences below using the words in brackets. (Use capital letters where necessary)

1. In the past, I smoked, but I stopped a few months ago. (used)
- , but I stopped a few months ago.
2. I planned to get up early this morning, but my alarm didn't go off. (going)
- this morning, but my alarm didn't go off.
3. Emily would work out at the gym every day before she got married. (used)
- at the gym every day before she got married.
4. My parents wanted to travel by plane, but all the flights were fully booked. (going)
- , but all the flights were fully booked.
5. Our Maths teacher always helped us whenever we couldn't solve a problem. (would)
- us whenever we couldn't solve a problem.
